How to Play 🎯

1
Find people who match each square on your board. Each square is a human trait, skill, achievement, or recognition.
2
Negotiate a price. You start with 1,000 points. When you find a match, agree on a price with them.
3
Buy their answer — tap the square, expand it, hit Buy. Snap a selfie together as proof. You spend points.
4
Sell yourself — when someone wants to buy from you, tap the square and hit Sell. You earn points.
5
Complete lines to unlock your score multiplier. Lines = rows, columns, and diagonals.
